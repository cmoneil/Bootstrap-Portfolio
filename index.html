About Me

I grew up and have lived in the Midwest my whole life. I guess Minnesotans don't really consider themselves Midwesterners but everyone in Minnesota thinks I'm from the south because I lived in Kansas. Kansas is not in the south. Its right in the middle of the country. Though anytime I go back to visit there, I seem to pick up a "southern accent". Its not an Alabama southern accent but its definitely not a Minnesota accent. I have now lived in Minnesota longer than anywhere else so I guess I am now more Minnesotan than anything.

The picture to the left was taken in Portovenere, Italy. It's right on the coast of Italy and is just to the south of Cinque Terre. My wife and I went there and the end of our honeymoon in the summer of 2017. We did a whirlwind European honeymoon. It was 2 weeks but we managed to go to 6 different cities so it was long but we crammed in a lot of Europe. It was definitely the best trip of my life. I have had some really good trips before but this was my first as a husband and it made it that much better.

I attended the University of Kansas and graduated with a bachelor's degree in psychology. I have worked in advertising, banking, retail, and printing since graduation, but nothing directly related to my degree. I still follow everything KU related even the football team even if they may be the worst D1 football in the country. You would think that a school that has one of the best basketball programs in the country could at least field a decent football team.
